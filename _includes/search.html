Wildcards:
Include an asterisk (*) as a wildcard in your search term, e.g. foo*
Fields:
Restrict the search to a specific field using a colon (:) and search multiple terms by adding space between terms. e.g. title:Introduction author:Dubra
Boost:
Boost the importance of terms in multi-term searches with caret (^) followed by an integer representing importance, e.g. foo^10 bar^5 baz
Fuzzy Matches:
Allow fuzzy matching with a tilda (~) followed by an integer represting the word edit distance, e.g. foo~1
Term Presence:
Specify required and avoided terms with + and - , e.g. +foo bar -baz
